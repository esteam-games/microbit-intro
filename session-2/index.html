Game 1: Animated Pet 🐾

🐾

Your micro:bit pet listens to sound!

Press ↓ to learn more

Meet Your Digital Pet!

  • 🔇 Quiet room = Asleep (it's peaceful!)
  • 😲 Medium noise = Surprised (what's that sound?)
  • 😢 Very loud = Sad (too noisy!)
  • Your pet reacts to the sounds around it!

Can you keep your pet happy by staying quiet?

What you'll learn:

  • 🎤 Using the microphone / sound sensor
  • 🔀 Multiple if/else conditions
  • 😊 Displaying different animated faces
  • 🔄 Continuous checking with "forever" loop
Programming Concept: Sensors let your code react to the real world!

The Challenge! 🎤

Experiment Time:

  • 1️⃣ Stay perfectly quiet — watch your pet sleep peacefully!
  • 2️⃣ Talk normally — your pet looks surprised!
  • 3️⃣ Shout or clap loudly — your pet gets sad!

Can the whole class keep the pet happy for 10 seconds?

MakeCode Blocks 🧱

The blocks code

basic.forever(function () {
    let noise = input.soundLevel()
    if (noise > 150) {
        basic.showIcon(IconNames.Sad)
    } else if (noise > 60) {
        basic.showIcon(IconNames.Surprised)
    } else {
        basic.showIcon(IconNames.Asleep)
    }
    basic.pause(200)
})

Game 2: Fireworks Display 🎆

🎆

Create explosive light shows!

Press ↓ to learn more

How Fireworks Work

  • 🔘 Press Button A to launch!
  • 🚀 A single LED lights up — the rocket!
  • 💥 BOOM! A big pattern fills the whole screen
  • 🌑 Screen clears — ready for the next launch!

Programming Concepts

🔘 Button Event: Code runs when Button A is pressed
🎞️ Animation Frames: Show different LED patterns one after another
⏱️ Timing: Pauses (basic.pause) control how long each frame shows

MakeCode Blocks 🧱

The blocks code

input.onButtonPressed(Button.A, function () {
    // The rocket!
    basic.showLeds(`
        . . . . .
        . . . . .
        . . # . .
        . . . . .
        . . . . .
    `)
    basic.pause(300)

    // BOOM!
    basic.showLeds(`
        # . # . #
        . # # # .
        # # # # #
        . # # # .
        # . # . #
    `)
    basic.pause(400)

    basic.clearScreen()
})

What is Animation? 🎬

🎞️

Press ↓ to discover the secret

The Secret of Animation

  • Animation = Many pictures shown quickly!
  • Each picture is called a "frame"
  • Show frames fast = things look like they move!
  • Movies show 24 frames per second

Our micro:bit has 25 LEDs (5x5 grid) to animate!

Dance Party!

  • 🧍 Create a stick figure using LEDs
  • 💃 Design different dance poses
  • 🔄 Switch between poses to animate dancing
  • 🎵 Add music to match the moves!

Button A = Dance Move 1 | Button B = Dance Move 2 | Shake = Random Dance!

What you'll learn:

  • 🎨 Designing custom LED patterns
  • 🎞️ Frame-by-frame animation
  • 🔘 Multiple button inputs
  • 🎵 Combining visuals with sound
Animation Tip: Small changes between frames = smooth movement!

MakeCode Blocks 🧱

The blocks code

// Dance pose 1 - Arms up
function pose1() {
    basic.showLeds(`
        # . # . #
        . # # # .
        . . # . .
        . # . # .
        . # . # .
    `)
}

// Dance pose 2 - Arms down
function pose2() {
    basic.showLeds(`
        . . # . .
        . # # # .
        # . # . #
        . # . # .
        . # . # .
    `)
}

// Dance pose 3 - Lean right
function pose3() {
    basic.showLeds(`
        . . # . .
        . . # # #
        . # # . .
        . # . # .
        # . . . #
    `)
}

input.onButtonPressed(Button.A, function () {
    pose1()
    music.playTone(262, 200)
})

input.onButtonPressed(Button.B, function () {
    pose2()
    music.playTone(330, 200)
})

input.onGesture(Gesture.Shake, function () {
    for (let i = 0; i < 4; i++) {
        pose1()
        music.playTone(262, 150)
        basic.pause(200)
        pose2()
        music.playTone(330, 150)
        basic.pause(200)
        pose3()
        music.playTone(392, 150)
        basic.pause(200)
    }
})
